# Alert

## Usage
<Playground>
  <Alert>
    Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.
  </Alert>
</Playground>

---

## Closable
You may add either the `<Alert.Close />` component or the `closable` prop to make it closable.
<Playground>
  <Alert>
    <Alert.Close />
    Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.
  </Alert>
  <Alert closable>
    Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.
  </Alert>
  <Alert closable>
    <Alert.Close />
    Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.
  </Alert>
</Playground>
